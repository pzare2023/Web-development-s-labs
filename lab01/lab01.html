My name is Parnia Zare

I was born on December 7th in Karaj, Iran. I’m 20 years old and currently living in Toronto, Canada. In 2018, I made a significant move to Canada, and since then, my life has been a blend of growth, adaptation, and ambition. Interestingly, I've moved 19 times in my life, which has shaped my resilience and adaptability.

I am a 4th-year Computer Science student at Toronto Metropolitan University. My academic journey began in biology, but during my first year, I realized that my passion lay elsewhere, prompting me to switch majors. The decision to transition into computer science has opened a world of possibilities for me, fueling my curiosity in technology, programming, and innovation.

This year, my primary focus is on maintaining a strong GPA while building meaningful networks that will guide my future career. I’m driven to explore different pathways in the tech world, aiming to find the one that aligns with my interests and aspirations post-graduation.

Aside from my academic pursuits, I find joy in art and fashion, as they allow me to express creativity in ways that complement my logical and analytical side. I also cherish the autumn season, as it brings a sense of calm and reflection amidst the whirlwind of life. As I look ahead, I’m eager to discover where my passion for computer science, combined with my diverse interests, will lead me.
