🎀 Lab 03 🎀

The goal of lab 03 was to make a responsive web page. I was assigned to achieve this in two different methods, part A was with using CSS fluid grids and part B was with using Bootstrap. In part A, I had challenges finding the right maximum and minimum width for different devices such as phone, tablet and laptop. I also had to make sure my styles were not getting over ridden throughout the different media screens. On the other hand part B was very straightforward, needed less coding, easy to follow. It took me less time to implement the layout I had in mind. Having the option to edit the different media screen sizes layouts next to each other made my work much easier and I liked it the best.
